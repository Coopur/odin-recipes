Hashbrown Plate

Description

Above features a hashbrown plate comprised of eggs, hashbrowns and bacon. It is very simple but super good and has tons of protein.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Hashbrowns

Steps

  1. Grab however many eggs, bacon and hashbrowns you want to use
  2. On one side of the grill turn it on high and set the hashbrowns on it
  3. On the other side turn the grill on between low and medium and put the bacon on it
  4. Every 3 minutes or so flip the hashbrowns to make sure they get really good and crispy
  5. Let the bacon cook for a while they will normally be good about when the hashbrowns are done
  6. Once the hashbrowns and bacon are done put the bacon on the side where the hashbrowns are
  7. Turn the side of the grill that the bacon used to be on to the high setting
  8. Crack your eggs on to that side
  9. Let the eggs sit for a little then flip them
  10. You can repeat this process until the eggs are cooked to your liking
  11. Once done get a plate and put the hashbrowns on then stack the bacon and the fried eggs onto the hashbrowns
  12. Grab your fork and enjoy your simple meal!
