Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Above features a breakfast sandwich comprised of toast, eggs, ham, bacon and cheese. It will surely beat any breakfast sandwich you have had before.

Ingredients

  • Sandwich bread
  • Ham
  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Grab however many eggs, bacon and ham you want to use
  2. Put your bread in the toaster but don't put it down yet
  3. Turn one side of the grill onto the high setting
  4. Lay your bacon and ham on the grill
  5. Every so often flip it back and forth until it is cooked to your liking
  6. Once done put the bacon and ham onto the other side of the grill and turn it onto the low setting
  7. Get your eggs and crack them onto the grill that the bacon and ham was previously on
  8. To time it correctly, once all of your eggs are cracked go pop the bread down in the toaster
  9. Every so often flip your eggs until they are cooked to your liking
  10. Go inside and grab your toast
  11. Start off by putting half the bacon and half the ham you made on the toast
  12. Then sprinkle the mozzarella cheese onto the ham
  13. Layer the eggs on top of the ham
  14. Put the other half of bacon and ham on top of the eggs
  15. Put the other piece of toast on top
  16. You now have an amazing breakfast sandwich enjoy!
