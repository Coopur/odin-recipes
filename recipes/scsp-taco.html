Steak,Chicken,Shrimp, and Pineapple Taco

Description

Below features a taco comprised of steak,chicken, shrimp and pineapple. Steak, chicken and shrimp are great together but the addition of grilled pineapple just makes the taco taste even better.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Chicken Breast
  • Shrimp
  • Pineapple
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

    Fill bowl up with hot water and thaw shrimp inside
    Cut up steak and chicken
    Grab however much pineapple you want and put in bowl
    Put chicken, steak and shrimp onto grill on high setting
    Put your tortillas on the other side of the grill on the medium setting
    Let your meat cook about 75% of the way then put the pineapple on with it
    Once one side of the tortilla is good and crispy flip it and sprinkle mozzarella cheese on it
    The cheese will melt some on the tortilla
    Once the meat is cooked to your liking and the tortilla looks good simply put the meat and pineapple into the tortillas
    You can sprinkle some cheese on top of everything after if you would like
    Enjoy!
